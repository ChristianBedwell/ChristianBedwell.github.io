Spotify Share App

July 2019 - Present

Having recently acquired the skill-set for Android development, I created the Spotify Share App to apply what I have learned and to create an app that would be useful to me, since I am a hard-core music enthusiast. Essentially, the Spotify Share App authenticates an Android user with a REST API, allows the user to query their top artists and tracks with parameters entered via TextViews/Spinners, and displays the results in a RecyclerView populated with card views. The user may also click on each query result to view an artist/track in detail and share it with a friend.

This was made possible by applying the latest technologies in software development, while emphasizing on an Internet-connected device.

Arduino Controlled Laser-Pointer Device

August 2018 - December 2018

The ultimate goal of this project was to fulfill a need in a scenario where an indoors pet cat was not always occupied and provide a means to keep that cat from finding activities to do that were otherwise damaging to household furniture and/or electronics. When cats play, not only is it mentally stimulating, but it also keeps cats fit and in shape. Cats enjoy stalking, chasing, and pouncing on objects. The laser pointer device projects a red dot onto a surface and dances around for the cat to chase. The laser pointer's directional movement is provisioned by a pan and tilt kit, and is controlled via Android device over a Bluetooth connection.
